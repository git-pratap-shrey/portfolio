About Me

I'm an AI-focused backend developer specializing in LLM-powered and agentic systems. My passion lies in building systems that are not just intelligent, but architecturally sound — scalable pipelines that can retrieve, reason, and respond with precision.

I designed and built uniAI, a syllabus-aware AI assistant that retrieves answers from academic PDFs using embeddings and vector databases — a full end-to-end production RAG system. I also won 1st Place at Changethon, IIT Roorkee for building Aurora Sentinels, a vision-language environmental monitoring system.

My interests span GenAI systems, AI agents, LLM infrastructure, and applied machine learning. I love the intersection of systems thinking and AI — building pipelines that work reliably at scale, with smart retrieval and structured outputs.
